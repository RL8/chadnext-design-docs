Song Similarity System

How we find songs that sound and feel alike

What Is It?

Think of it like "Customers who liked this also liked..." but for Taylor Swift songs.

The Song Similarity System analyzes every song in Taylor's discography and figures out which songs are most similar to each other based on their vibe (mood) and sound (audio features).

üí° In One Sentence: A pre-calculated lookup table that stores "how similar" every Taylor Swift song is to every other song, based on both their emotional vibe (60%) and musical characteristics (40%).

How It Works

Convert Songs to Numbers

Each song is turned into two sets of numbers (vectors) that capture its essence:

Mood Profile

4 Numbers
Mood type, energy level, emotional intensity, tone

Audio Features

6 Numbers
Danceability, energy, valence, acousticness, instrumentalness, speechiness

Compare Using Math

We use Cosine Similarity to measure how close two songs are to each other. Think of it as measuring the angle between two vectors.

Combine the Scores

We weight mood similarity more heavily because vibe matters more than just the sound:

Final Score = (Mood √ó 0.6) + (Audio √ó 0.4)

Store for Fast Lookup

All scores are pre-calculated and saved in a database table called song_similarities. When you click a song, we just look up the top 10 most similar songs instantly!
